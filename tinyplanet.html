Tiny Planet

Created using Unity Game Engine and C# scripting, this climate change-focused video game helps educate kids and teens about environmentally conscious practices. The objective is to score as high as possible in the mini-games to preserve the planet. There are three mini-games: water conservation, deforestation, and energy conservation. Player scores in mini-games will have an overall effect on the game planet. Game has a login system and leaderboard to view the highest scores in each mini-game.
